Switching Dog Food

You have to change diets gradually. If you switch dog food too quickly, it can cause vomiting, diarrhea, or gas. Dogs eat the same diet for a long time, so their digestive system isn't used to different food.

When you are switching dry dog food, decrease the amount of dog food you give your dog. Measure with a cup the amount to give your dog. Add food to help digesting. Pumpkins and probiotics should be added. Feed half of the usual amount you give your dog.


Good dog food has meat,vegetables, grains, and fruit. If your dog begs for food, don't give them your attention. People's food could be bad for dogs.
