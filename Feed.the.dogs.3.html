Eating Grass

If your dog is eating grass, don't worry. Most of the time, it's because of boredom, especially in young dogs. Most veterinarians think it is a normal dog behavior. 79% of dogs eat grass. Dogs who eat grass usually don't throw up.


Dogs could be eating grass because they are bored. Make sure your dog gets enough exercise. Your dog could need more nutrition, so try a different kind of dog food.

Herbicides and pesticides could be harmful if your dog swallows them with the grass. Some plants could be harmful, so make sure your dog stays away from them.
