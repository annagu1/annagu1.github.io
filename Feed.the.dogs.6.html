Bad Dog Food

Bad dog food has bad ingredients, which is bad for dogs. Bad dog food could have roadkill, zoo animals, or meat wrapped in plastic. You can avoid that by not buying dog food that says "meat meal" or "meat". Chicken and chicken meal are better than "chicken by-product". Bad dog food could cause digestive problems and allergic reactions. It is bad for mental health.
